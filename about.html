I'm Christine Okazaki, a designer going into my last year at UC Davis, studying Design and Psychology. I was born and raised in the Bay Area, but I’m constantly daydreaming about what the rest of the world looks like. I’m a strong advocate for the iterative design process and the power of a carefully chosen font combination.
