TYPOGRAPHY

  • x TYPOGRAPHY

Typography is an area of design I am particularly interested in; it is an essential part of most visuals, and has the ability to completely change the feeling of a message. I am fascinated by the endless possibilities one can create just by adjusting colors and typefaces. These are some examples of posters I have done from song lyrics and quotes. I wanted to include variety throughout my pieces, which I achieved by approaching each poster with a different color palette and typeface.
